About

Hello! I’m Marija, a dedicated Data Analyst with a background in Software and Data Engineering. My journey in tech began as a software tester, where I gained valuable experience in analyzing systems, identifying issues, and collaborating with development teams to improve product quality. This role sharpened my attention to detail and taught me the importance of structured problem-solving—skills that I now apply in data analysis. I like transforming complex datasets into actionable insights using tools like SQL, Python (Pandas), Power BI, Tableau, and Excel.

My passion for data drives me to constantly learn and improve, and I am always excited to tackle new challenges in data analysis and engineering. Whether it’s creating interactive dashboards or building efficient data pipelines, I’m committed to making data work for businesses by uncovering insights that support smarter decisions.

In my free time, I enjoy learning new languages, traveling, playing volleyball and exploring different cultures. Currently, I am learning Spanish and looking forward to applying my skills in international settings.
